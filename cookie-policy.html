Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

🍪 Good news — this website currently uses no cookies and sets no tracking of any kind.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They're used for various purposes including keeping you logged in, remembering preferences, and tracking behaviour for analytics or advertising.

Our current approach

The Stafferton Digital website is a static site with no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking scripts. We do not set any cookies on your device when you visit this site.

The only data processed when you visit is standard server log data handled by Cloudflare (our hosting provider) for infrastructure and security purposes. This is not used to identify or profile individual visitors.

If we add analytics in future

We may add privacy-respecting analytics in future — for example, using GA4 configured with analytics storage denied by default, which does not set identifying cookies. If we introduce any cookies that require your consent, we will update this policy and add an appropriate consent mechanism before doing so.

Third-party services

Our contact form is processed via Cloudflare Pages Functions and MailChannels. Neither service sets cookies in your browser as part of this process.

Contact

Questions about cookies or data? Email hello@stafferton.digital or see our Privacy Policy for full details on how we handle personal data.
